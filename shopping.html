Shopping

The best places to go shopping in Manchester range from major shopping centres and designer jewellery boutiques to charming indoor markets and outlet stores. Manchester is widely known for its sporting history, but there are ample places to pick up a nice souvenir or two. The city is home to some of the UK's largest malls, where you can find a good mix of high-street, upmarket, and affordable stores.

 

Manchester is a hub of culture, entertainment, sport, and tourism – and its great shopping scene follows suit. Some neighbourhoods are known for specific goods, such as jewellery, local art, antiques, and vinyl records. Whichever you're planning to snag a bargain or indulge in upmarket shopping, you can be assured of the extensive range of shopping opportunities in Manchester
